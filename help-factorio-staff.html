gavelModerator commands

  • Send a player to the jail/banish area/jail <playerName>
  • Recover a player from jail/unjail <playerName>
  • Override a vote-banish/overrrule <playerName>
    • To clear ALL votes: /overrule clear
  • See votes for banish/votes
  • Reset player level & score/reset <playerName>
  • Promote player to member/member <playerName>
  • Promote player to regular/regular <playerName>
  • Promote player to veteran/veteran <playerName>
  • Revive a player in one-life / permadeath mode/onelife <playerName>

routeTeleportation

    Location formats: <x,y> or <surfaceName> or [gps=x,y] or [gps=x,y,surface]
  • Go to to a player/goto <playerName>
  • Teleport to a location/tp <location>
  • Summon a player to you/summon <playerName>
  • Transport a player to a location/transport <playerName> <location>
  • See a list of surface-names/surfaces

speedAdjust speeds

  • Set game speed in UPS/aspeed <game UPS>
  • Set game speed as multiplier/gspeed 1.0
  • Adjust your run speed, 0 (normal) through 10 (very fast)/run 0

starMisc

  • Move spawn to self/cspawn
    • Optional: <x, y>
  • Toggle immortatility/immortal
    • Optional: <playerName>, otherwise defaults to self.
  • Reveal <units> area from map center <0,0>/reveal <units>
  • Clear map pins and fog of war/rechart
