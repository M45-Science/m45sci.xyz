Improve Your Connection from Europe

If you're connecting from Europe and notice lag, stuttering, or disconnections while playing on M45-Science servers, you can use the EU Proxy system to improve your connection.

Our Factorio servers are hosted in Michigan, USA (near Chicago and the Great Lakes) by incx. The EU proxy servers are located in Nuremberg, Germany, hosted by Hetzner.

The proxy routes your traffic through a stable TCP tunnel from Europe to the US, reducing:

  • Packet loss
  • Transatlantic latency spikes
  • Disconnections caused by bad ISP routing

Optional Proxy Software

You can also run our optional proxy software on your computer. Unlike the EU relay servers on this page, it creates a direct encrypted tunnel from your PC to the M45-Science servers in Michigan.

This can help stabilize your connection if you're dealing with:

  • WiFi instability
  • CGNAT or router issues
  • Weird ISP routing
